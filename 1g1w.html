One Game a Week Jam

Make small things. Finish them.

1G1W is a recurring game jam built around momentum: make small, playable games, finish them, share them, and learn by shipping.

Jam Status Leaderboard Join Discord

Intentions

Build scope discipline through repetition.

Each week gives creators a compact set of prompts. The goal is not to build a masterpiece every time; the goal is to practice finishing, publishing, and learning from small playable work.

  • Start fresh during the jam period.
  • Keep the game playable and scoped small.
  • Use the prompts creatively.
  • Share constructive feedback with other participants.
